FAQ

How safe is my data?

Very. We take data protection very seriously and confidential information cannot be intercepted. We will never sell or share your private information with third parties no matter how inviting the party looks.

What happens after the free trial ends?

We'll be good friends by then and we'll contact you to arrange payment - you will incur no costs without your approval.

What commitment am I making?

You only use your pledge when you find projects that suit your ambitions. neighbourly does not hold your money and you can change your pledge at any time via the dashboard.
